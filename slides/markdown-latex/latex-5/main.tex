\chapter*{Introduction}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Introduction}
This document serves as a comprehensive study guide for the course "Stochastic Finance and Risk Modelling". It expands upon the lecture slides relative to Chapter 5: "Multi-period model of financial market". 

The goal of this text is to provide a rigorous mathematical foundation required for modern financial modeling in a multi-period discrete-time setting. We extend the concepts from one-period models to dynamic multi-period models. We cover the setup of the discrete-time market, the formal definition of self-financing strategies and portfolios, arbitrage opportunities, martingale measures, and the pricing and replication of European derivatives. The text concludes with an analysis of market completeness.
