\chapter*{Introduction}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Introduction}
This document serves as a comprehensive study guide for the course "Stochastic Finance and Risk Modelling". It expands upon the lecture slides relative to Chapter 1: "From probability to stochastic process". 

The goal of this text is to provide a rigorous mathematical foundation required for modern financial modeling. We begin with the axiomatic definition of probability spaces and $\sigma$-algebras, move through random variables and their distributions, and finally establish key convergence results and the concept of conditional expectation, which is central to martingale theory and asset pricing.
